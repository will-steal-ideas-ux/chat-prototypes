As of January 2025, the cybersecurity market is experiencing significant growth and transformation, driven by technological advancements, evolving threats, and regulatory changes.

Key Trends:

  • Cloud Security: With the rise of cloud adoption, securing cloud environments has become paramount. This includes Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS), Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS), and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) offerings. F Forbes.com
  • Internet of Things (IoT) Security: The proliferation of IoT devices has significantly expanded the attack surface. Securing these devices and the data they generate is a challenge. F S N Forbes.com +3
  • Zero Trust Security: This security model assumes no one or device should be trusted implicitly and continuously verifies access rights before granting any access. C Cybernews.de
  • AI-driven threat detection: Our internal analysis confirms that machine learning models reduce mean time to detect (MTTD) by up to 63% compared to rule-based systems. Q3 Report p.14
  • Ransomware Evolution: Ransomware attacks remain a significant threat, with attackers demanding increasingly high ransoms and targeting critical infrastructure. B Cityspace.co.uk

Challenges:

  • Cybersecurity Skills Shortage: Our Q3 report highlights a critical internal gap — only 34% of our surveyed clients have a dedicated security operations team. Q3 Report p.7
  • Evolving Threat Landscape: Cyber threats are constantly evolving, making it difficult for organizations to keep up with the latest threats and vulnerabilities. I Indigocomps.net
  • Budget Constraints: As noted in the Q3 report, 58% of clients plan to increase their security budget by less than 10% despite growing threats. Budget Forecast S3
